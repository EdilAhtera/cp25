PT Dil Healthcare Indonesia

PT Dil Healthcare Indonesia, also known by the brand name Compimedic Solutions, is a leading medical device distributor in Indonesia. As a healthcare solution provider, the company focuses on distributing high-quality medical products from trusted global manufacturing brands. With a commitment to the best service and innovation in the healthcare sector, Compimedic Solutions continues to strive to meet the needs of the Indonesian market with medical device products that meet international standards, as well as providing reliable technical support and customer service to hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare facilities throughout Indonesia.
